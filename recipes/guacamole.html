Guacamole

Description

Fresh, creamy, and loaded with flavor, this guacamole is the perfect dip for chips, tacos, or any Mexican-inspired dish.

Made with ripe avocados, lime juice, and a few simple mix-ins, it's a quick and healthy snack or side.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop into a bowl.
  2. Mash with a fork to desired consistency.
  3. Mix in onion, garlic, tomato, and lime juice.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and optional cilantro.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate with plastic wrap pressed on the surface to avoid browning.
