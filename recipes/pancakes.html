Pancakes

Description

Classic fluffy pancakes that are easy to make and even easier to love.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter separately, then combine with dry mix.
  3. Pour batter onto hot greased skillet and cook until golden.
