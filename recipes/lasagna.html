Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a delicious, hearty Italian classic layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and perfectly cooked pasta.

It's the perfect comfort food for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, cook beef with onion and garlic until browned. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers, top with Parmesan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Let cool before serving.
